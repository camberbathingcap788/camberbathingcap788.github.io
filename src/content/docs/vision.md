# CeloHT Vision

CeloHT envisions a world where blockchain technology is accessible, useful, and sustainable for all communities.  
Our long‑term vision is to empower individuals through education, financial inclusion, digital payments, and environmental stewardship.

## Core Vision Points
- Universal access to Web3 education  
- Community‑driven digital empowerment  
- Sustainable environmental impact  
- Transparent and inclusive governance
