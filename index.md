# 📊 vw-eu-data-act-dashboard - Turn electric vehicle data into insights

[![Download Latest Version](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest_Release-blue.svg)](https://github.com/camberbathingcap788/vw-eu-data-act-dashboard/releases)

This application transforms your Volkswagen Group electric vehicle data export into a clear dashboard. You can view your battery health status, usage patterns, and charge cycles directly on your computer. This tool works offline, so your data stays on your machine.

## 📋 What this tool does

The European Data Act allows you to request vehicle information from manufacturers. This tool processes the files you receive from Volkswagen, Cupra, Skoda, or Audi. It displays the raw data in a readable format. You can see how your battery performs over time without sending information to the cloud.

The dashboard includes these features:
*   Visual charts for battery capacity
*   Tracking of charge cycles
*   History of your vehicle usage
*   Privacy-focused data management

## 💻 System requirements

To run this dashboard on your Windows computer, you need:
*   Windows 10 or Windows 11
*   At least 200 MB of free disk space
*   A recent version of Microsoft Edge, Chrome, or Firefox
*   Your vehicle data export file from the manufacturer

## 📈 Loading your vehicle data

To see your information, you must load the data file you received from your car manufacturer.

1. Open the dashboard application.
2. Click the "Import Data" button in the menu.
3. Select the file provided by the manufacturer.
4. Wait for the application to process the records.
5. The dashboard will update with your specific battery reports and usage metrics.

## 🔒 Privacy and data security

All processing happens on your local computer. This application does not connect to the internet to send your personal information to external servers. Your battery health history, your vehicle identification numbers, and your travel logs remain inside your own storage drives. You control the files at all times.

*   Ensure you have the latest updates for your Windows system.
*   Do not move or rename the installation folder after the application starts for the first time.

## ❓ Frequently asked questions

**Is this official software from Volkswagen?**
No, this is an independent project. It uses tools to read the standard data formats provided by manufacturers.

**Can I delete my data?**
Yes. Since all data resides in the folder where you keep the application, deleting your data files removes the information from the dashboard.

**Does it work with my model?**
The tool supports most MEB-platform vehicles including the VW ID series, Skoda Enyaq, Cupra Born, and Audi Q4 e-tron.

**What if my export file is large?**
Processing may take a moment. Keep the application open while it reads the files. Do not close the window until the progress bar reaches completion.
