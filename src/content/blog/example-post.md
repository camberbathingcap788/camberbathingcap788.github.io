# Understanding cUSD and Digital Payments

cUSD is a stable digital currency built on the Celo blockchain. It enables fast, low‑cost, and borderless payments for communities worldwide.

## Why cUSD Matters
- Stable value  
- Works globally  
- Accessible through mobile wallets like Valora  
- Ideal for education, merchants, and community agents  

## How CeloHT Uses cUSD
CeloHT integrates cUSD into education programs, agent network operations, and community financial inclusion initiatives.

cUSD is a cornerstone of CeloHT’s mission to empower communities through modern digital finance.
